## ğŸ“‹ Complete Workflow

### Step-by-Step Process:

1. **Select Patient**
   - Choose from your appointment list
   - Patient info auto-fills

2. **Enter Diagnosis**
   - Type or use voice recording
   - Review transcribed text
   - Play audio to verify

3. **Select Symptoms**
   - Click applicable symptoms
   - Add custom symptoms if needed
   - Review selected symptoms

4. **Add Medications**
   - Click "Add Medication" for each medicine
   - Select medicine from dropdown
   - Enter dosage (e.g., "500mg")
   - Enter frequency (e.g., "Twice daily")
   - Enter duration (e.g., "7 days")
   - Record or type instructions
   - Play audio to verify

5. **Additional Information**
   - Add any extra notes
   - Provide follow-up instructions

6. **Submit**
   - Review all information
   - Click "Create Prescription"
   - System saves and notifies patient
